Superior Chemical Resistance

HDPE pipes resist a wide range of chemicals, acids, and alkalis. Unlike metal pipes, they won't corrode, rust, or scale, ensuring pure water quality and extended service life in aggressive environments.

Exceptional Flexibility & Durability

High flexibility allows for easier installation in challenging terrains. Combined with durability, our pipes withstand ground movement and stress without cracking.

Leak-Proof Fusion Joints

Butt fusion and electrofusion joints create monolithic systems with no weak points, eliminating leaks and reducing maintenance costs over the pipeline's lifetime.

50+ Year Service Life

Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Extrusion

HDPE granules are melted and extruded through a die to form the pipe profile. Temperature and speed are tightly controlled for consistency.

  • Twin-screw extruders
  • Precise temperature zones

Cooling

Controlled cooling in water baths or spray systems ensures uniform crystallinity and prevents deformation.

  • Vacuum calibration
  • Gradual cooling
